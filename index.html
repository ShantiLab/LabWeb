About Us

We Study the Building Blocks of Life to Tackle Health and Science Challenges

At our IISER Berhampur lab, we study the shapes and roles of molecules to support advances in medicine, drug development, and biotechnology.
Exploring Molecular Structures

We study the shapes of molecules like proteins and DNA using tools like X-rays and electron microscopes. This helps us understand diseases and design targeted medicines./p>

Using Computers to Predict Molecule Behavior

We combine lab experiments with computer models to predict how molecules act, guiding new experiments and aiding in drug discovery.

Studying Molecular Interactions

We examine how molecules connect, fold, and change shape to understand their stability—key for developing effective medicines.

Where We Are

Our Lab at IISER Berhampur

Our lab is located at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Berhampur, situated in the serene region of Ganjam, Odisha. The campus is nestled along the coastline, offering a peaceful environment that inspires learning and research.

IISER Berhampur is equipped with modern laboratories and research facilities, providing students and researchers with the tools they need to explore and innovate. The institute fosters a vibrant academic community, encouraging collaboration and the pursuit of scientific excellence.

The natural beauty surrounding the institute adds to the overall charm, making it a unique place to study and work.
